Advertising Balloons and Blimps

Drive traffic to your location with attention grabbing advertising balloons, blimps and air dancers from Balloon Adventures of Sacramento. Perfect for housing developments, retail stores, special events, grand openings and any event or occasion you wish to promote in a BIG and exciting way.

Products

  • Helium Blimps

    Our helium blimps are 13-feet long and fly high above your business, capturing attention from a distance and leading customers directly to you. Available in a variety of colors and includes a short message or logo on both sides of the blimp body for additional impact. We provide the helium and set-up, too!

  • Air Dancers

    These whimsical dancers come in a variety of colors and styles to create a fun and inviting environment. We deliver the dancers to you and provide set-up and take down service.
